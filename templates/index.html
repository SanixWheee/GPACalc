GPAWIZARD

About!

A quick and easy way to calculate your weighted & unweighted GPA in seconds!

Features!

We have an interactive AI Assistant for all of your questions regarding grades! We also allow you to export your grades + GPA in a PDF Format!

How to Use!

Step 1: Create an account and login

Step 2: Go to your dashboard

Step 3: Press the '+' on the respective grade to add a class taken that year

Step 4: fill out the form and press 'add class'

Step 5: Repeat steps 3-4 until you have put all the classes you want

Step 6: Press 'Download PDF' to download your GPA table
